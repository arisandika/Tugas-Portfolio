1011 Psikotes

1011 Psikotes is an intuitive platform for candidates to take psychological assessments, helping HR teams make better recruitment decisions.

1011 Psikotes

Fullstack Development

1011 Psikotes is a dedicated platform designed for prospective employees to participate in psychometric evaluations as part of the recruitment process. Offering tests like Papikostick, DISC, and Enneagram, the platform helps candidates showcase their personality traits, behavioral tendencies, and work preferences. The results from these assessments serve as essential tools for HR teams, providing valuable insights to guide the next steps in the hiring process.
